About Me

I'm a data analyst who enjoys taking messy, real-world data — sports stats, API feeds, business operations numbers — and turning it into something clear enough to act on. My projects walk through the full path from a raw CSV or API response to a cleaned dataset, an analysis, and a chart or dashboard that actually answers a question.

I like working in public: every notebook in my Projects section is written so someone else could follow the same steps, not just see the final chart.
